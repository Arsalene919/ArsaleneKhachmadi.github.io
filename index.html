Hi — I'm Arsalene Khachmadi, a graduated data scientist based in Antibes, France. I specialise in building end-to-end ML systems: from exploratory analysis and feature engineering through to training, evaluation, and production deployment.

I'm particularly interested in how language models can be grounded in domain-specific knowledge, and how causal methods can help us go beyond correlation to make better decisions.

Outside of work, I contribute to open-source, write about statistics on my blog, and spend too much time in Kaggle competitions.
