About Me

Hi, I'm Arsalene Khachmadi, a data scientist based in Antibes, France. I recently graduated with a Master's in Intelligent Communication Systems from EURECOM and an Engineering Degree in Data Science from ESPRIT.

I'm drawn to the intersection of language models and real-world decision-making — whether benchmarking LLMs for causal reasoning, engineering fraud signals from transactional data, or building NLP pipelines on messy, noisy corpora.

I have a growing interest in applying AI to fraud detection and life sciences, and I care deeply about responsible AI development. I thrive in agile, multicultural environments and speak Arabic, French, and English fluently.
